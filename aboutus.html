Who We Are

At FoodIQ, we believe the most dangerous ingredient in any kitchen is uncertainty.

For decades, canteens and hostels have operated on guesswork. How much rice to boil? How many rotis to bake? The result is a daily tragedy: perfectly good food ending up in the bin while millions go hungry.

We are a team of engineers and sustainability advocates building the digital infrastructure to stop this.

Our Approach

  • Real-time Tracking

    Digitizing meal lifecycles to eliminate human error.

  • Data-Driven Decisions

    Actionable insights help managers cook exactly what is needed.

  • Seamless Donation

    Instantly connect surplus food with local NGOs.
